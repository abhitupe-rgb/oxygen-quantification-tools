📖 Glossary — key terms explained

PSA Plant
Pressure Swing Adsorption — a machine that makes oxygen on-site from ambient air using electricity.
LMO
Liquid Medical Oxygen — oxygen stored at very low temperature in a tank, refilled by tanker deliveries.
D / B Cylinder
Portable compressed oxygen cylinders. D-type holds ~7,000 L; B-type holds ~1,700 L.
Cost per litre (₹/L)
The total monthly cost of a source divided by the litres it produces — the key comparison metric.
LPM
Litres Per Minute — how fast the PSA plant produces oxygen.
OPEX
Operational Expenditure — all running costs combined (electricity + staff + maintenance + fixed fees).
IPD
Inpatient Department — patients admitted overnight, who are the primary oxygen consumers.
kWh
Kilowatt-hour — a unit of electricity; multiply kW by hours to get kWh consumed.
m³
Cubic metre of gas (= 1,000 litres). LMO is priced per m³.
